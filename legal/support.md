## FAQ

### Where is my data stored?

All timeline data is stored locally on your device. TimeMaxx does not use a backend or cloud storage in this version.

---

### Does TimeMaxx collect my data?

No. TimeMaxx does not collect or transmit your personal or timeline data.

---

### How do I delete all my data?

Open **Settings → Reset All Data**.

Deleting the app will also remove all locally stored data.

---

### Can I recover my data after deleting it?

No. Since data is stored only on your device and not backed up by TimeMaxx, deleted data cannot be recovered.

---

### I changed categories and want the defaults back

You can:

* Manually edit categories in Settings
* Or reset all app data to restore defaults

---

### The app isn’t working as expected

Try the following:

* Restart the app
* Restart your device
* Update to the latest version

If the issue persists, please contact support with details.
