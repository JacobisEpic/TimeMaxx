Last updated: February 25, 2026

## Contact

## What to include in bug reports
- App version
- Device model
- iOS version
- Reproduction steps
- Screenshots/video if available

## FAQ
### Where is my data stored?
All timeline data is stored locally on-device.

### How do I clear everything?
Open Settings and use "Reset all data".

### I changed categories and want defaults back.
Use Settings to edit categories manually, or reset app data.
